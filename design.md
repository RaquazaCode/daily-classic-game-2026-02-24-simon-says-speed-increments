# Design: Simon Says (Speed Increments)

## Core Fantasy
Memorize and replay a growing light sequence on a four-pad console while the tempo accelerates each round.

## Twist
Every completed round increases the playback tempo, shortening the flash and gap durations. This raises difficulty while also boosting score rewards.

## Controls
- Mouse click pads or press keys `1`-`4`.
- `P` toggles pause.
- `R` restarts the run.
- `Enter` starts from the intro screen.

## UX Notes
- A minimal overlay introduces controls and offers a scripted demo.
- In-game HUD is limited to round, score, and tempo indicators.
- Result overlay appears on failure and prompts restart.

## Visual Direction
Warm retro console palette, high-contrast pads, and subtle glow on the active pad. The board sits inside a dark frame to keep focus on the sequence.
